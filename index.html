Grow a Healthy Garden with Bugs

Insects are important because of their diversity, ecological role, and influence on agriculture, human health, and natural resources.

Friendly Insects Information

Bees are known for their role in pollination and for producing honey.
Butterflies have a four-stage life cycle and undergo complete metamorphosis.
Many dragonflies have brilliant iridescent colours, making them conspicuous in flight.
Grasshoppers have powerful hind legs which allow them to escape from threats by leaping vigorously.
